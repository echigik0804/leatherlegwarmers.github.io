Leather Care & Styling

Extend the life of your leather leg warmers and keep them looking rich and supple with simple care habits.

Care Instructions

  • Spot clean with a dedicated leather cleaner; avoid soaking the leather.
  • Wipe with a slightly damp, soft cloth to remove surface dust after wear.
  • Condition occasionally with a quality leather conditioner to maintain softness.
  • Allow to air‑dry away from direct heat if exposed to moisture.
  • Store flat or gently rolled to avoid hard creases in the leather.

How to Wear

  • Slip over pumps, booties, or ankle boots for an instant tall‑boot look.
  • Style with mini dresses, skirts, or skinny denim to highlight the full length.
  • Pair with evening wear for a sleek, high‑fashion finish or with streetwear for bold contrast.
