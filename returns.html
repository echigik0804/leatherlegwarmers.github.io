Return Policy

We want you to love your Leather Leg Warmers. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, you may request a return under the conditions below.

Return Window

Returns must be requested within 7 days of delivery.

Eligibility

Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition with all original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

Custom-sized or made-to-order items are final sale and cannot be returned.

How to Start a Return

Email us at info@leatherlegwarmers.com with your order number to request return instructions.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.
